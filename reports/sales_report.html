⚠ Re-attempt Gap Too Long

Average gap between 1st and 2nd attempt is 159.7 hours (~6.6 days). Best practice for warm outreach is 24–48 hours. This gap suggests contacts are not being systematically re-worked.

⚠ Single-Touch Problem

55.9% of contacts were called only once. Given a 32.2% first-attempt connect rate, over 800 contacts who weren't reached on attempt 1 were never followed up on.

✓ Diminishing Returns Clear

Data clearly shows the optimal ceiling is attempts 3–4. Beyond attempt 5, connect rates drop below 16% and the marginal return does not justify the time cost.

→ Recommended Attempt Limit

3–4 attempts per contact within a structured cadence (Day 1, Day 2, Day 4–5). After 4 attempts with no connection, move to email/social or archive the lead.
